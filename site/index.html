Vision

To act as a seamless conduit between the Institute and Industry, identifying the requirements of the industry, mapping them with industry readiness and core competencies of students, identifying and filling the gaps through enhanced training so as to make them relevant with the global industrial needs

Mission

To assist the holistic development of the students with balanced set of technical skills, interpersonal skills and with a positive attitude towards life and to be the interface between the Institute and Industry.

Industry Institute Interaction

Working effectively and efficiently, T & P liaises with various industries and associations to facilitate training, getting live projects as well as providing best possible placement opportunity to the students. The department also takes care to ensure active interaction with the industry by organizing guest lecturers/seminars/interactive sessions with HR managers of reputed companies and Alumni

Campus Recruitment Training(CRT)

With the objective to meet industry requirements and to create quality human resource, a comprehensive 250 hours Campus Recruitment Training(CRT) is provided to every student right from first semester. This training spans from 1st to 7th semester and is imparted by professionals.

Message from Director Placements

We consider it to be an honor to extend a very warm welcome to all the potential recruiter organizations to Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering (YCCE), Nagpur. YCCE has a legacy of more than 35 years as one of the premier institutes of Central India. To ensure the quality education, YCCE always go through the accreditation process of National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The institute is awarded with ‘A’ Grade of by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) for a period of five years 2016-2021. In the recent survey conducted by CMR-Dataquest Magazine March 2020, YCCE stands All India Rank 32nd (including Govt. & Self Finance Institutes) and 4th in West Zone of India. YCCE has maintained its position always in Top 150 colleges across engineering colleges in the country in NIRF, MHRD, Govt. of India An 110 hour Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) is provided to all the 6th Semester students in between 6th and 7th semester during summer vacation. Before the CRT, Pre-Assessment Test is conducted by reputed professional assessing agencies and the results are shared with students to know his/her strength & weakness about Aptitude Test (English/Quantitative Techniques/Logical Reasoning, Domain Knowledge & Coding/Programming) After the CRT, Post-Assessment test is again conducted to check improvement of the students. The modules of the CRT are regularly validated by Senior HR professionals of Top MNC's.
